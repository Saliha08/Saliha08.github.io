Who We Are

Helping Hands is a volunteer-driven organization founded in 2010. We believe in the power of community and work to create opportunities for everyone to give back and grow.

Our team is a diverse group of individuals passionate about creating positive change — whether it’s helping a family in need or organizing neighborhood clean-ups.

Core Values

  • Community Empowerment
  • Inclusivity & Respect
  • Transparency & Accountability
  • Education & Growth
  • Environmental Stewardship
